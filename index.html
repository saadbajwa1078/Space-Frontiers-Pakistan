About the Conference

Pakistan's Premier Space Conference

Space Frontiers Pakistan is a four-day international conference bringing together leading scientists, engineers, entrepreneurs, and policy makers to explore the frontiers of space technology and its applications for Pakistan and the world.

Hosted by Nexus Events and supported by PakSat, SUPARCO, and international partners, this conference is a unique platform for knowledge sharing, networking, and collaboration.
