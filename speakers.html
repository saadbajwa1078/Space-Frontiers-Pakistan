World-Class Experts

Keynote Speakers

Thought leaders from space agencies, universities, private sector, and government shaping the future of space.

DR
Opening Keynote Speaker

Dr. Rabia Sarfraz

Director, Space & Upper Atmosphere Research Commission (SUPARCO)

Ph.D. Aerospace Engineering, MIT

Dr. Rabia Sarfraz has been at the forefront of Pakistan's space programme for over two decades. Her groundbreaking research in satellite imaging has enabled agricultural monitoring across 85% of Pakistan's farmland. She spearheads Pakistan's ambition to launch its own moon mission by 2035.

Pakistan's Role in Global Space Exploration
AK

Ahmed Karim

CEO, Orbital One Pakistan

MSc Aerospace Engineering, University of Surrey

Pioneer of Pakistan's commercial space sector. Ahmed founded Orbital One at 28, launching Pakistan's first commercial imaging satellite in 2023. He advocates for a thriving local space startup ecosystem.

Private Sector in LEO Satellites
FN

Dr. Fatima Noor

Associate Professor, NUST

Ph.D. Remote Sensing, TU Delft

Leading researcher in remote sensing and earth observation. Dr. Noor's work on precision agriculture has helped 500,000+ farmers in Punjab optimise crop yields using satellite data.

Remote Sensing for Agriculture
MH

Muhammad Hassan

CTO, PakSat International

BSc Electronics, UET Lahore; MSc Telecommunications, BUET

Technical architect of Pakistan's next-generation satellite communication infrastructure. Muhammad is leading the transition from Ku-band to Ka-band for high-throughput internet services across Pakistan.

Next-Gen Satellite Communications
AM

Dr. Ayesha Malik

Special Adviser, Ministry of Science & Technology

Ph.D. Science Policy, Oxford

Dr. Malik is instrumental in drafting Pakistan's first National Space Policy. She represents Pakistan in UN COPUOS and advocates for equitable access to space for developing nations.

Space Policy & Regulation
IK

Dr. Imran Khan

Research Scientist, ESA (European Space Agency)

Ph.D. Astrophysics, University of Cambridge

Pakistani-born scientist at ESA's Earth Observation Directorate. Dr. Imran's climate monitoring research using the Copernicus programme has received global recognition for its accuracy and impact.

Earth Observation & Climate
ZA

Zara Ahmad

Founder, SpaceTech PK

BSc Computer Science, LUMS

Pakistan's most celebrated space entrepreneur. Zara's startup SpaceTech PK builds miniaturised satellite components for the global market. She was named Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia in 2025.

Space Entrepreneurship
SR

Sultan Rizvi

Director General, Pakistan Air Force Research & Development

MSc Avionics, Cranfield University

A decorated Air Commodore with 25+ years in aerospace R&D. Sultan has led classified satellite navigation projects that support Pakistan's defence and civilian infrastructure.

Aerospace R&D & Defence
NB

Dr. Nadia Baig

Professor, Institute of Space & Planetary Astrophysics, UoK

Ph.D. Planetary Science, Caltech

Pakistan's foremost planetary scientist. Dr. Nadia leads an international consortium studying Mars geology using data from NASA's Perseverance rover. She inspires thousands of students through public outreach.

Planetary Science & Astrobiology
